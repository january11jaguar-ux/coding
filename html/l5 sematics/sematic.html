1 facts about dogs

Dogs have a sense of time but do not understand it the same way humans do. They cannot measure minutes or hours, but they can remember daily routines and anticipate events, such as when it is time for a walk or meal.

2 Their history

Dogs were domesticated between 20,000 and 40,000 years ago, making them one of the first domesticated animals.

3 Their behavior

Dogs are known for their loyalty and companionship. They can be trained to perform various tasks and are often used as service animals.

Did you know?

Dogs have been known to help humans in various tasks, from herding livestock to providing companionship and assistance to those in need.
